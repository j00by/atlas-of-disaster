Map Information

Days with dangerously high temperatures are becoming more frequent in the United States as the planet gets hotter. This map shows you where temperatures are rising fastest and makes it easy to reach your elected officials.

Methodology

We matched projected climate change data provided by Probable Futures and Woodwell Climate Research Center to U.S. counties.
